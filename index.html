A Few Words About ACM

We're Functioning for Almost 0 Years

UPES-ACM Student Chapter, established in 2010, was introduced to address the needs of the UPES computing community.

The aim of this chapter is to create an interest among the students of computer science for their stream from apart what they learn from their curriculum.

The chapter organizes seminars and workshops on different subjects by speakers who are well-versed in their area of expertise. Apart from these, various technical and non-technical events are also organized by the chapter.
