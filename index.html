Technical Orientation

All journeys start with a plan. To make a plan you need information. Our journey therefore starts with tech orientation.

Workshops

Only knowing isn't enough, we must do if we want to truly move forward. In lieu of this thought we've decided to have two full day hands-on workshops. Just For You.

Prep-a-thon

Knowing is using your knowledge to help, to create. We end our weekend with Prepathon, a hackathon for the future hackathons.

Potential of a CS student - Introductory talk Git & GitHub Development Competitive programming Projects:college vs industry Research Design

Potential of a CS student - Introductory talk

The introductory session is to help students understand their potential as a CS student and how their skills can benefit society. A motivational talk by an alumni, it's aimed at encouraging students to develop their interests in such a way that benefits both themselves as well as the tech industry.

Git & GitHub

An introductory session on Git and Github, the talk will emphasize on the importance and understanding of Github as well as the difference between the two. The talk will also feature a session where the audience will be guided through the basics of using Github. A little time will also be devoted to make the audience aware of GSoC, Google Summer of Code.

Development

A 3-hour session on what the development of a software entails. The talk will cover both the back-end development such as deployment and front-end development like creating the website and Android applications. A walk through of the ACM app will also be given during the session.

Competitive programming

A session to impart knowledge to the students about Competitive coding and it's importance. It will cover the basics of CP, how to get started and why it is a necessary skill. Those in attendance will also be made aware of the top programming contests such as ICPC, Codejam, Kick-start etc. and how to take part in them.

Projects:college vs industry

This session will focus on the importance of projects at both the college and industry level. It'll also cover the basics of how to choose a project and the right way to build it based upon the organization, contest or any other situation you're creating it for.

Research

A short session on Research papers and how to go about working on one. The talk will cover the basics of writing a research paper, how to find legitimate sources and the importance of knowing how to write one.

Design

The session will include a brief overview on the various categories of Design field including UI/UX, VFX, Web design etc. It is aimed at guiding the audience those who are interested in Design to chose the right option.

A Few Words About ACM

We're Functioning for Almost 0 Years

UPES-ACM Student Chapter, established in 2010, was introduced to address the needs of the UPES computing community.

The aim of this chapter is to create an interest among the students of computer science for their stream from apart what they learn from their curriculum.

The chapter organizes seminars and workshops on different subjects by speakers who are well-versed in their area of expertise. Apart from these, various technical and non-technical events are also organized by the chapter.
